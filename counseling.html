Empowering Women Through Supportive Counseling

Specialized therapeutic services designed to help women navigate life's challenges, heal from trauma, and discover their inner strength in a safe, confidential environment.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive counseling options tailored to your unique needs and preferences, all delivered by trauma-informed professionals.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one sessions focused on your personal journey, providing a confidential space to address specific challenges, trauma, and growth opportunities. Our therapists specialize in women's issues including anxiety, depression, trauma recovery, relationship concerns, and life transitions.

  • Personalized treatment plans
  • Flexible scheduling options
  • Virtual and in-person sessions available

Group Therapy

Supportive group sessions where women can connect, share experiences, and heal together. Our facilitated groups provide a powerful environment for growth through shared understanding and collective wisdom in a safe, structured setting.

  • Trauma recovery groups
  • Life transitions support
  • Mindfulness and stress reduction

Our Trauma-Informed Approach

At Women's Counseling Services, we recognize that many women have experienced trauma in various forms. Our trauma-informed approach acknowledges this reality and shapes everything we do—from how our office is designed to how we interact with each client.

Safety First

We create physical and emotional environments where you feel secure, respected, and in control of your healing journey.

Trust & Transparency

We build relationships based on clear communication, consistency, and respect for your boundaries and choices.

Empowerment

We focus on recognizing your strengths, supporting your agency, and collaborating with you as the expert of your own experience.
