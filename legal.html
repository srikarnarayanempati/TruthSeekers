Legal Support Services

Free and reduced-cost legal services for protection orders, custody, and more. Dedicated to supporting women in crisis situations.

Our Legal Services

Protection Orders

Immediate legal protection from abusers through restraining orders and no-contact orders. Our attorneys can help file emergency protection orders within 24 hours.

Child Custody

Legal representation for securing custody arrangements that prioritize child safety and wellbeing. Includes emergency custody petitions in crisis situations.

Domestic Violence Cases

Comprehensive legal support for survivors of domestic violence, including criminal proceedings, civil remedies, and connections to support services.

Immigration Support

Specialized legal assistance for immigrant women facing abuse, including VAWA petitions, U-Visas, T-Visas, and asylum applications based on domestic violence.

Family Law

Assistance with divorce proceedings, child support, spousal support, and property division with special consideration for situations involving abuse or control.

Housing & Financial Security

Legal help with housing issues, debt protection, and financial recovery for women leaving abusive situations, including lease termination and credit repair.
